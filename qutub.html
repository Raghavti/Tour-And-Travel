QutubMinar

Qutub Minar, a towering masterpiece in the southern part of Delhi, epitomizes India's rich history. Rising majestically, it is a testament to intricate Islamic architecture dating back to the 12th century. Each intricately carved tier tells a story of bygone eras, making it a symbol of the city's grand past. As a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Qutub Minar stands not only as an architectural marvel but also as a living testament to Delhi's cultural richness and historical significance.
